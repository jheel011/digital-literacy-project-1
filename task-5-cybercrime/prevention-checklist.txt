Stay Safe Online – Checklist

- Use strong and unique passwords for all your accounts and update them regularly
- Never share your OTP, passwords, or personal information with anyone
- Enable two-factor authentication (2FA) for better account security
- Avoid clicking on suspicious links or downloading files from unknown sources
- Always check the authenticity of websites before entering sensitive information
- Be cautious while using public Wi-Fi, especially for banking or personal activities
- Keep your devices and applications updated with the latest security patches
- Think before you share personal information on social media platforms

UPI / Financial Safety:
- Never enter your UPI PIN to receive money; it is only required when sending money
- Do not accept unknown payment requests or scan QR codes from untrusted sources
- Always verify the receiver’s details before making any online payment

Reporting Channels:
- Report cybercrime at: https://cybercrime.gov.in
- Call Cyber Crime Helpline: 1930
